Ingredients

  • 2 hot dog buns
  • 2 Frankfurt sausages
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • Lemon and olive oil to taste
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Cut the avocado in half lengthwise, remove the pit, peel it, and dice it.
  2. Mash the avocado to make a puree, adding a bit of salt, pepper, a few drops of lemon juice, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Steam the sausage for 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Carefully cut the bun, but do not separate it completely; leave it in a butterfly shape.
  5. Place the sausage at the bottom of the bun.
  6. Spread a layer of avocado puree on top.
  7. Add a layer of mayonnaise over the avocado.
  8. Finish with the diced tomato on top.
